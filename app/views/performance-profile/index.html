Export trainee data for the {{ previousAcademicYear }} performance profile sign off

Signing off your list of trainees for the performance profiles publication deadline is 31 January 2025.

As an accredited provider, you need to sign off your teacher trainee data from the previous 2023 to 2024 academic year.

If you’re in a partnership, you’re also responsible for ensuring your lead partners’ trainee data is accurate and signed off by the deadline.

The data you sign off will be included in the annual initial teacher training performance profiles publication. This will be published on GOV.UK.

Finding your trainee data

You need to check all registered trainees who studied in the previous 2023 to 2024 academic year.

Export 2023-2024 trainee data (237 trainees).

Checking your trainee data

You’ll need to check that all your trainees from the 2023 to 2024 academic year are listed. For each trainee you’ll need to check:

  • course details
  • status
  • the date they were awarded qualified teacher status (QTS) or early years teacher status (EYTS), or deferred or withdrew from the course - unless they’re still studying
  • 2 placements for trainees that were QTS awarded

Fixing mistakes in your trainee data

You’ll need to fix any mistakes before you sign off the data.
