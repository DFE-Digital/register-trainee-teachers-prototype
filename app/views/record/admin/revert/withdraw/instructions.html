Withdrawals can be reverted where:

  • the trainee was withdrawn in error
  • the trainee withdrew and then changed their mind, and is now continuing on the same course

Withdrawals cannot be reverted where the trainee will be returning to a new course. If this has happened, instruct the provider to register the trainee again on a new course.
