To get set up on Register trainee teachers (Register) you need a DfE Sign-in account.

Get a DfE Sign-in account if you do not already have one.

Then request an account for Register trainee teachers by sending an email to becomingateacher@digital.education.gov.uk

The email address associated with your DfE Sign-in and Register account must:

  • be a named email address, for example jane.doe@example.com
  • have an email domain associated with your organisation, we cannot accept emails that end with @gmail or @icloud

In your email, tell us:

  • the name of your training provider or Lead school
  • that you would like to start using Register trainee teachers
  • the email address associated with your DfE Sign-in account

We will email you once we have set up your account.
