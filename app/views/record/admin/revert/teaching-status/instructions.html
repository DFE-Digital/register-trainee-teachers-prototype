Reverting a teaching status is a significant step. DQT may need to contact the trainee to destroy their certificate.

Providers should be asked to provide sufficient justification for why a teaching status needs to be reverted.

{# {{ govukInsetText({ text: "This trainee holds both QTS and EYTS. Check you are reverting the correct record." }) }} #}

Instructions for reverting teaching status:

  1. Ask provider to confirm they are sure
  2. Check you have found the correct trainee in Register
  3. Contact DQT to revert the trainee
  4. Wait until DQT confirms the update before making changes in Register
  5. Make the change in Register
  6. Notify the provdier that the change has been made
