Use this export to see new starter trainee data you have provided for the {{academicYear}} academic year.

What you must do

ITT Providers must sign-off that they have provided accurate and up to date data for new starters.

You can use this report to review the data you have provided to DfE to check for errors and omissions.

When your organisation is happy to sign-off, a responsible officer should use use this form to notify DfE. Your organisation must sign-off your data no later than 31 October {{academicYearShort}}

How this relates to the ITT census

DfE asks that you sign off all new starter data, not just those included in the ITT census.

The ITT census is put together using the Initial Teacher Training Census methodology.

About this export

This export includes all trainees:

  • with a start academic year in {{ academicYear }}
  • with an ITT course start date on or before the census date
  • with a trainee start date on or before the census date
  • where you have yet to provide a trainee start date
