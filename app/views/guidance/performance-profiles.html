This is so that the data can be included in the annual initial teacher training performance profiles publication. This will be published on GOV.UK. ## Find your trainee data You need a list of all registered trainees who studied in the 2021 to 2022 academic year. You can get this by either: - downloading a [report with the data you need to check](/reports/performance-profiles) - exporting a [list of registered trainees filtered to show the 2021 to 2022 academic year](/records?%5BfilterAcademicYears%5D=2021+to+2022) ## Check your trainee data Check that all your 2021 to 2022 trainees are listed. For each trainee you should check: - course details - status - the date they were awarded QTS or EYTS, deferred or withdrew from the course, unless they are still studying ## Fix mistakes in your trainee data You need to fix any mistakes before you sign off the data. How you do this depends on whether your organisation is a: - provider of school-centred initial teacher training (SCITT) - higher education institution (HEI) ### If your organisation is a SCITT You should: - email becomingateacher@digital.education.gov.uk if the trainee either withdrew or has been awarded QTS or EYTS - make the change in this service if the trainee is still studying or has deferred ### If your organisation is an HEI For trainees who were registered through HESA, you should: - email becomingateacher@digital.education.gov.uk if the trainee is no longer studying - make the change through HESA if the trainee is still studying For trainees who were not registered through HESA, you should: - email becomingateacher@digital.education.gov.uk if the trainee either withdrew or has been awarded QTS or EYTS - make the change in this service if the trainee is still studying or has deferred ## Sign off your trainee data A senior person from your organisation must [complete a form to sign off the data](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeOKcMGCzt4E_mlfUJl07PBbRq8Gnn6jIq_m2pv0WsnzcrKXQ/viewform?usp=sf_link). This should not be the same person who entered the data. {% endblock %}
